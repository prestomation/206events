Free, community-powered calendar of Seattle-area cycling events.
Anyone can post a ride; many local clubs and individuals do
(Cat Rides NW, Good Weather, Eastside Urbanism, Kidical Mass,
Coffee Outside Seattle, Boots & Toby's Walks, etc.).

ICS feed lives at `https://everydayrides.com/cities/seattle-wa/calendar.ics`
(linked from the public `/calendar/subscribe` page). Live fetch from the
Claude Code web environment returned a valid VCALENDAR with **108 events**
through summer 2026 (sample: Boots & Toby's Waterfront Walk, Eastside
Sunday Ride, Coffee Outside Seattle, Beer Junction Bike Club, Bicycle
Weekend rides, Round 3 | Wednesday Night World Champs 2026, etc.).

Discovered while investigating a Bike Rodeo poster for May 30, 2026 at
Seward Park (Rainier Valley Safe Streets / Family Bike Seattle). The
specific rodeo wasn't in the feed yet, but EverydayRides is exactly the
kind of community aggregator that picks up these events when organizers
post them — it's tangential to the original lookup but a strong source
on its own merits.
