Annual free community festival and parade in the Lake City neighborhood
(Lake City Way from 135th to 125th, then west on 125th to 25th Ave NE).
2026 edition: Saturday, August 1, 10:30am–9pm. Live bands, live art, a car
show, a free petting zoo, art & craft vendors, multi-cultural food trucks,
and an evening parade (Kids' Parade 6pm, Dog Parade 6:30pm, Grand Parade
7pm).
