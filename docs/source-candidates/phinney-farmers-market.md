**Phinney Farmers Market** — `https://seattlefarmersmarkets.org/pfm` — 6761 Phinney Ave N, Phinney Ridge — Tags: FarmersMarket, Phinney, Greenwood

Seasonal Friday afternoon farmers market run by Neighborhood Farmers Markets (SFMA) in partnership with the Phinney Neighborhood Association. Serves the Phinney Ridge and Greenwood communities from the upper parking lot of the PNA.

Schedule: Fridays, 3pm–7pm, June 5 – September 25, 2026. Also hosts a Harvest Market on November 20.
