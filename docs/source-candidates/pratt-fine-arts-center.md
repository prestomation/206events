**Pratt Fine Arts Center** — `https://pratt.org/` — community art studio and gallery at 1902 2nd Ave, Belltown (main) with additional locations. Offers metalworking, painting, ceramics, and glass arts classes to the public; hosts opening receptions, Art Parties, and markets.

Investigated 2026-06-30:
- `/events` URL returns HTTP 404 — events are managed through their Canvas platform (`canvas.pratt.org/classes`) rather than the main site
- Uses **ARTdynamix® by Dream Warrior Group** for the main site CMS (same proprietary platform as Gage Academy of Art)
- Canvas (`canvas.pratt.org`) is a class registration system, not a public calendar with ICS export

**Verdict:** Not viable — no public ICS/API. Their events and classes are managed through a proprietary registration system. Revisit if they add a public-facing calendar (e.g., Eventbrite or Tribe Events) for public programs.
