Annual 3-day community street festival in The Junction, organized by
WS Junction (45th edition in 2026, July 9-12). No ICS/API/JSON feed —
just static marketing pages on `wsjunction.org` and a separate
`westseattlesummerfest.com` site. No consistent "Nth weekend" date
pattern to support a recurring YAML entry (dates shift year to year).
Single annual multi-day event with no machine-readable schedule of
sub-events (stages/times) — not viable as either a ripper or recurring
source.
