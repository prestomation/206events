The Stroum Jewish Community Center is located at 3801 E Mercer Way on Mercer Island (not Seattle proper).

Investigated 2026-06-21:
- WordPress site with Tribe Events plugin confirmed
- ICS feed accessible: `https://www.sjcc.org/events?post_type=tribe_events&ical=1`
- 30+ upcoming events confirmed
- **All events are at SJCC on Mercer Island, not in Seattle**
- Event types: member-only fitness classes (Pilates, Aqua Fit, Zumba) — primarily private member programming
- Very few (if any) public community events; mostly indoor fitness classes

**Verdict**: Not viable — (1) located on Mercer Island, outside Seattle, and (2) events are primarily member-only fitness classes, not the type of public community events 206.events focuses on.
