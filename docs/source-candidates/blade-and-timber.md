a "Seattle axe throwing" search.

Investigated 2026-07-24:
- Site is a Netlify-hosted app; the location page fetched without JS
  rendering returned a 404 shell rather than content
- The venue is fundamentally a reservation/booking business (private
  lane bookings, birthday parties, corporate events) rather than a
  publicly-listed events calendar — no evidence of a public schedule of
  open-to-the-public events to scrape
- Contrast with Axe Kickers (`docs/source-candidates/axe-kickers.md`),
  which does run public league nights and tournaments

**Verdict**: Not viable — no public events calendar, primarily a private
booking venue.
