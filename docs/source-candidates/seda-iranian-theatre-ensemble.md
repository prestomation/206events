Discovered from a community poster board photo — "Wish You Were Here" by
Sanaz Toossi, June 11 – July 5 2026. (The poster's "Reza" reading is
actually **Seda** — *voice* in Persian — Seattle's first Iranian theatre
company, founded 2019.)

Seda's own Squarespace site lists only *past* productions; "Wish You Were
Here" isn't on it, and there's no ICS/API/upcoming feed. The show is a
**co-production hosted at ArtsWest** (4711 California Ave SW, West
Seattle). ArtsWest event pages carry JSON-LD, but per-performance
showtimes still require the JS-rendered Salesforce ticketing frontend —
ArtsWest is already evaluated `notviable` for that reason (see
`artswest.md`). Verified 2026-05-29.

No viable automated source. ArtsWest programming (including Seda
co-productions and the separately-confirmed "Aviatrix") currently
surfaces via the SEAtoday aggregator.
