Public park at 2101 N Northlake Way, Seattle, WA 98103.
City of Seattle Parks & Recreation does not maintain a per-park event
calendar. Events are listed on Do206 (do206.com/venues/gas-works-park)
but that's an aggregator, not a primary source. Special events require
city permits and are announced ad hoc. No viable scrapeable source.
