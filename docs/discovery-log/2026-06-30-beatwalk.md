## New source added

### Columbia City Beatwalk — implemented as recurring

- **URL:** https://beatwalk.org
- **Tags:** Music, Columbia City, Community
- **Status:** Added as `sources/recurring/columbia-city-beatwalk.yaml`

Free outdoor live music festival series in Columbia City's historic commercial
district. Multiple stages along Rainier Ave S feature diverse genres (Latin,
jazz, R&B, hip-hop, world music, roots). Runs every 2nd Sunday, June through
September. Confirmed active in 2026 (June 7 date confirmed).

The site uses Modern Events Calendar (MEC) Lite (WordPress), but the plugin's
events only contain 2025 placeholder content. Implemented as a recurring YAML
instead since the schedule pattern is well-established: `2nd Sunday`,
`months: [6, 7, 8, 9]`, `start_time: "16:00"`, `duration: PT8H`.

RRULE: `FREQ=MONTHLY;BYDAY=2SU;BYMONTH=6,7,8,9`, DTSTART July 12, 2026 at 4 PM.
