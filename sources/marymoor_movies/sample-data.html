Bring Your Own Seating

Movies at Marymoor offer bring-your-own general admission seating. Seating is first-come-first-served using the following guidelines:
Section 1: Blankets and ground seating only (no raised chairs)
Section 2: Low-back / sand chairs and ground seating only (no camp chairs)
Section 3: Open seating, camp chairs, sand chairs and ground seating

These are walk-in sit-in-the-grass events.

Bicycling to Movies at Marymoor

We welcome all bicyclers but no bikes inside the event. Please lock your bike along the south fence line before enter through the main entrance on the west.

Dog Friendly

Outdoor Movies at Marymoor Park events are dog friendly. Please bring your nice furry friends to the movies and make sure to be a responsible owner by keeping your dog on a leash at all times. Please clean up after your dog.

Food Trucks

Get your foodie on with some of Seattle and Redmond’s best food trucks at the Movies at Marymoor. Cupcakes, popcorn, candy, tacos, burgers, grilled cheese, seafood, pizza, salads and more. Click each event to see which trucks will be attending.

Outside food and beverages are allowed (no alcohol).

Entertainment

Join us for pre-movie entertainment and activities while you picnic and enjoy the food trucks prior to each movie.

DJs | Live Music | Karaoke | Photo Ops | Yard Games | Sponsors Free Give Aways... Click each event to see the evening entertainers.

And we always encourage movie themed dress-up for attendees.

Vendors

Movies at Marymoor events have a selection of vendor activities and food trucks before each movie. Come early, get your spots and enjoy our vendors and event activities. If you're interested in promoting your company as a vendor at the Movies at Marymoor please contact us today to learn more.

Rain or Shine

Outdoor Movies at Marymoor Park are rain or shine in most cases. Any changes to scheduled events will be posted first on our Facebook page.
