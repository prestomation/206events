Spring 2026
⚘ ⚘ ⚘ ⚘ ⚘ ⚘ ⚘ ⚘
Shop 180+ curated artists at our lovely springtime fair in Magnuson Park!

Applications for this event are open until March 22!

Sign up for our mailing list to be notified about applications ✉

Join us for a weekend of small business revelry! Renegade offers an answer for conscious consumerism, sustainability, and an accessible creative economy. We aim for low-impact, thoughtful, and highly curated events that are joyful for artists & shoppers alike. Come one, come all!

If you would like to support this and future events, a suggested $5 entry fee can be paid via QR code onsite. Your contribution supports Renegade Craft’s vision of an accessible, equitable, & inclusive creative economy for all.

Hangar 30 @ Magnuson Park

6310 NE 74th St.
Seattle, WA 98115

  • This Fair is indoors with some outdoor food trucks, and will go on rain or shine.
  • Service animals are welcome! Sorry, your adorable pets are not permitted.

Land Honoring

We acknowledge that Renegade Seattle is on native Coast Salish, Stillaguamish, Duwamish, and Suquamish land, and that tribal peoples live and work among us today. We commit to honoring the land and its Indigenous stewards by tending to the space with reverence and respect through a sustainable and low-waste event.

Transportation
We encourage carpooling, ride services, biking, or taking public transportation to the Fair.

Parking
Closest parking to the venue is located at lot W6 listed here.

Hangar 30 @ Magnuson Park is entirely ground level and accessible, including two accessible parking spaces near the hangar entrance.

There will be limited outdoor seating available near the entrance.

Service dogs are allowed into the Fair.

Please feel free to contact us with any further accessibility questions you may have.
