There is nothing quite like LIVE music in the summer on Lake Union.

Join us for Dinner on the Docks with Sugartime Trio, a 21+ waterfront gathering celebrating The Center for Wooden Boats’ 50th Anniversary season with cold drinks, great food, live music, and community in the heart of our historic floating campus.

With dinner by Ravishing Radish Catering, drinks, music by Sugartime Trio, and a one-of-a-kind waterfront setting, this is a perfect way to spend a summer evening on the docks and raise a glass to 50 years of hands-on maritime heritage.

Thursday, July 23, 2026
6:00–9:00 PM
The Center for Wooden Boats

21+ only
Advance reservations required

Food & Drink
Your ticket includes a Mediterranean buffet prepared by Ravishing Radish Catering, along with a variety of beer, red and white wine, and non-alcoholic beverages.

Community-Supported Ticket Pricing

To help make this event accessible to more guests, we are offering tiered ticket pricing.
