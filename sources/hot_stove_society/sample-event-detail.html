Date Night: NOLA Crawfish Etouffee with Chef Sean McFadyen

July 10 2026 | 5:30 PM - 7:30 PM

2000 4th Ave, Seattle, WA 98121

Transport your senses straight to the heart of New Orleans as Chef Sean McFadyen brings the soulful flavors of the Big Easy to Seattle. In this demonstration-style class, you’ll learn how to build a deeply flavorful crawfish étouffée from the ground up—starting with a rich, slow-cooked roux layered with the “holy trinity” of Cajun cooking, and finished with tender crawfish simmered in a velvety, spice-kissed sauce.

Along the way, we’ll explore the techniques that give this iconic dish its signature depth—balanced heat, savory richness, and that unmistakable Louisiana comfort. You’ll watch as plump scallops are seared to a perfect golden crust, their naturally sweet, buttery centers paired with creamy, slightly smoky corn maque choux bursting with fresh herbs and peppers. Then, dive into the star of the show: a luscious crawfish étouffée, where tender crawfish are smothered in a silky, deeply seasoned sauce—rich with caramelized aromatics, gentle heat, and layers of savory flavor that soak beautifully into fluffy rice. As you watch and learn, you’ll enjoy a curated tasting menu inspired by classic New Orleans cuisine, each dish designed to highlight bold spices, smoky meats, and a touch of Southern sweetness.

Guests will be welcomed with Smoky Grilled Andouille Sausage paired with bright, tangy Pickled Okra, finished with Creole Salt and a creamy, zesty Remoulade—setting the tone for a menu full of vibrant contrast and flavor. While guests enjoy the demonstration you will receive substantial tastes of the following menu items.
